## Product Name

Dark Mode for Chrome

## Short Description

Dark mode, midnight black, and black & white reading modes for websites in Chrome.

## Long Description

Dark Mode for Chrome helps make bright websites easier to read by applying a cleaner, more comfortable viewing experience across the web.

Choose the visual mode that fits your eyes and environment:

- Classic Dark for balanced everyday browsing
- Midnight Black for deeper contrast at night
- Black & White for distraction-free reading

Features:

- Dark mode for websites across Chrome
- Multiple reading modes
- Per-site pause and resume
- Daily schedule controls
- Quick popup access
- Custom new tab experience

This extension is designed for users who want better readability, less glare, and a more comfortable night browsing experience.

## FAQ Copy

### Does it work on every page?

It works on most websites, but Chrome blocks extensions from styling some protected pages such as the Chrome Web Store and internal `chrome://` pages.

### What is the difference between the modes?

Classic Dark is the standard balanced mode, Midnight Black is deeper and higher contrast, and Black & White removes color for focused reading.

### Can I disable it on specific sites?

Yes. You can pause the extension on the current site and manage paused sites from the popup.
